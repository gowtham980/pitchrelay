# Unity Arena — Accessibility

## Wheelchair & mobility
- All primary gates (A–E) are wheelchair accessible.
- Elevator banks: North (elev-n1), East (elev-e1), South (elev-s1). Use elevators for floors 2–3.
- North ADA Ramp connects Gate A to North Concourse without stairs.
- Stairs-only shortcuts (Club Stairs, West stairs to Section 210 / Sensory Room) are **not** ADA paths.

## Companion & wheelchair seating
- **Section 142** — primary wheelchair pads + companion seats (East Bowl).
- **Section 210** — upper west accessible via East Elevator Bank.
- **Section 305** — club level via North Elevator (not Club Stairs).

## Sensory & quiet
- **Sensory Room** (floor 2, west) — low stimulus, dimmable lights, seating for decompression.
- Access via East Elevator Bank (ADA). Avoid west stairs if mobility aids are used.

## Restrooms
- North ADA Restrooms and East Family Restroom are fully accessible.
- Adult changing table available in East Family Restroom.

## Service animals
- Service animals welcome in all public zones. Relief area near Gate E outer plaza (ask volunteer).

## Assistance
- Volunteer posts North and East can arrange escort.
- Request wheelchair loan at Gate A guest services (limited inventory).
