# Unity Arena — Sustainability

## Live meters (ops)
- **Energy (kW)**: stadium load including floodlights, HVAC, kitchens.
- **Waste fill %**: compactors at north and south service docks.
- **Transit share %**: estimated arrivals via Metro + shuttle vs private car.

## Match-day tips
- Open Gate D overflow early when north/east density high — shortens idle engine time at curb.
- Stagger post-match exits by section to smooth transit boarding and cut peak energy.
- Promote refillable water at North fountain; reduces single-use plastic at concessions.
- When waste fill >80%, divert concession packaging to south dock and alert cleaning crews.

- Decision Cards should include a one-line sustainability side-effect when recommending gate opens, hold patterns, or exit waves.
