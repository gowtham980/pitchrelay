# Unity Arena — Gates & Entry

## Gate A (North Primary)
- Primary fan entry for lower bowl north and club levels.
- Full ADA ramp + wide lanes; preferred for wheelchair users arriving from Metro.
- Peak congestion: 60–20 minutes pre-kickoff.
- Nearby: Volunteer Post North, North Market Hall, water fountains.

## Gate B (Northeast)
- Serves East Bowl and Section 142 companion seating.
- Medical Post B (AED) is 16 m inside the gate.
- Can back up when security screening is single-threaded; overflow may redirect to Gate C or Gate D.

## Gate C (Southeast)
- Secondary east entry; good for late arrivals when Gate B is congested.
- Connects to East Grab & Go and family restrooms.

## Gate D Overflow (South)
- Overflow / contingency gate. Open on ops decision during pre-match surge or transport waves.
- Best exit path toward Shuttle Bus Plaza.
- Keep clear for emergency vehicle access on the outer curb.

## Gate E (West)
- Closest gate to Metro Link Hub.
- Preferred arrival for transit-first fans and ADA users from rail.

## Entry tips
- Digital tickets only; have QR ready before screening.
- Bag policy: clear bags under published size; medical exceptions at any gate.
- Multilingual wayfinding screens at all gates (EN/ES/FR).
