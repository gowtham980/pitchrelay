# Unity Arena — Safety & Medical

## Medical posts
- **Medical Post B** (near Gate B): AED cabinet, trauma kit, dual-language staff roster.
- **South First Aid** (South Concourse): general first aid, hydration, cooling towels on hot days.

## AED
- Primary public AED: Medical Post B. Secondary kits at Ops Command Hub and South First Aid.
- For suspected cardiac event: start local volunteer response, radio ops, clear elevator path.

## Emergency exits
- East and West emergency exits — keep corridors clear; do not use for general egress unless directed.
- Field-level Pitch Ring is restricted; not a public evacuation path.

## Incident severity guide
- **Low**: wayfinding help, minor queue questions.
- **Med**: medical assist non-critical, localized congestion.
- **High**: extended gate queues, transport collapse, weather watch.
- **Critical**: mass casualty risk, severe weather hold, security threat — escalate immediately.

## Lost child / vulnerable person
- Hold in place if safe; notify nearest volunteer post; ops issues coded radio (not full PA name).
