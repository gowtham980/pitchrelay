# Unity Arena — Transport & Last Mile

## Metro Link Hub
- West side, ~40–45 m walk to Gate E (ADA path).
- Match-day frequency: every 6–10 minutes until T+90 after final whistle.
- Delays cascade into Gate E and West Bowl density — ops should watch hub ETA telemetry.

## Shuttle Bus Plaza
- South side near Gate D.
- Park-and-ride shuttles + accessible buses.
- Use when Metro is delayed; Gate D may open as overflow arrival.

## Rideshare
- Designated pickup on east curb beyond Emergency Exit East — do not block emergency lane.
- Post-match surge: expect 20–40 min waits; promote Metro/shuttle in PA.

## Bike & micromobility
- Valet racks north of Gate A. No e-scooters inside secure perimeter.

## Sustainability note
- Higher transit mode share reduces departure-wave energy and curb congestion.
- When transport delays >15 min, stagger PA exit messaging by bowl section.
